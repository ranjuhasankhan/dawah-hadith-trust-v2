About Us

Spreading the Light of Authentic Sunnah

«مَنْ يُرِدِ اللَّهُ بِهِ خَيْرًا يُفَقِّهْهُ فِي الدِّينِ»
"Whomever Allah intends good for, He gives them understanding of the religion."
— Sahih Al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim

Da'wah Hadith Trust is a globally recognized Islamic institution founded by الشيخ Hamza Al Hassan, dedicated to the preservation and dissemination of authentic Hadith sciences and Da'wah methodologies. Based in Bangladesh with centers in Dhaka and Sirajganj, our work spans 40+ countries.

Our programs are built on rigorous scholarly foundations, verified by senior Muhadditheen, and delivered through a modern approach that makes authentic Islamic knowledge accessible to Muslims worldwide — whether through in-person classes, online courses, or community outreach programs.

We are committed to empowering every Muslim — from the student of knowledge to the grassroots Da'ee — with the tools, training, and certification needed to spread the authentic Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

Hadith Collection

Prophetic Wisdom for Daily Life

Authentic narrations from the Sahih collections, guiding Muslims in every dimension of life — curated and verified by our scholars.

Hadith 001
«إِنَّمَا الْأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ وَإِنَّمَا لِكُلِّ امْرِئٍ مَا نَوَى»
"Actions are but by intentions, and each person will have but what they intended."
Sahih Al-Bukhari, Hadith 1 · Narrated by Umar ibn Al-Khattab (R.A)
Hadith 002
«خَيْرُكُمْ مَنْ تَعَلَّمَ الْقُرْآنَ وَعَلَّمَهُ»
"The best among you are those who learn the Quran and teach it to others."
Sahih Al-Bukhari, Hadith 5027 · Narrated by Uthman ibn Affan (R.A)
Hadith 003
«طَلَبُ الْعِلْمِ فَرِيضَةٌ عَلَى كُلِّ مُسْلِمٍ»
"Seeking knowledge is an obligation upon every Muslim."
Sunan Ibn Majah, Hadith 224 · Narrated by Anas ibn Malik (R.A)
Hadith 004
«الدِّينُ النَّصِيحَةُ»
"Religion is sincere advice — to Allah, His Book, His Messenger, and to Muslim leaders and the general Muslims."
Sahih Muslim, Hadith 55 · Narrated by Tamim Al-Dari (R.A)
Hadith 005
«مَنْ سَلَكَ طَرِيقًا يَلْتَمِسُ فِيهِ عِلْمًا سَهَّلَ اللَّهُ لَهُ طَرِيقًا إِلَى الْجَنَّةِ»
"Whoever treads a path seeking knowledge, Allah will make easy for him a path to Paradise."
Sahih Muslim, Hadith 2699 · Narrated by Abu Hurairah (R.A)
Hadith 006
«لَا يُؤْمِنُ أَحَدُكُمْ حَتَّى يُحِبَّ لِأَخِيهِ مَا يُحِبُّ لِنَفْسِهِ»
"None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself."
Sahih Al-Bukhari, Hadith 13 · Narrated by Anas ibn Malik (R.A)
